Organizations We Support

Full Basket Belize

The mission of Full Basket Belize (FBB) is to improve the education, health, and environment of Belizean communities and support youth and community economic development through scholarships and community grants.

FBB was founded in 2005 by way of a grassroots effort. Many retired peace corp volunteers began and continue to run this program, though as the word spreads about the support they provide the composition of their board of directors includes a wider variety of backgrounds.

As an organization that provides educational and community support by way of scholarships & grants, the intentions and effects of Full Basket Belize's work is well aligned with that of Access to Opportunity. Although the scope of AtO is not limited geographically, much of its beginnings were rooted in Belize where collaborating with FBB and networking with FBB's volunteers aided in the early growth of AtO.

San Pedro Tour Operators Association

Community-based organization that is actively creating opportunities for our current and future workforce. We have several programs that help us address the threats affecting our coral reefs. Our aim is to keep tourists coming to our island.
A Board of Directors, that is comprised of member tour operators, defines broad strategic direction and goals for the organization. They also ensure good leadership and strong board-functioning. A Program Director coordinates the implementation of our core programs with support from volunteers and collaborators.

Current Campaign

Belize Marine Ecosystem Education program (BMEE)

We've partnered with Reef Adventures Dive Shop and San Pedro Tour Operators Association to provide meals for the hard-working law enforcement in San Pedro, Belize who are out there all hours of each day helping to ensure the safety of the people by enforcing social distancing measures and shelter in place curfews for the COVID-19 virus.
